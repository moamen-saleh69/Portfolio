This project analyzes the post-pandemic "Tech Winter," tracking job losses across 1,000+ companies from 2020 to 2023. The goal was to identify industry trends, quantify the impact of major economic shifts, and visualize the "boom and bust" cycle of the tech sector.

## ğŸ” Key Questions Answered

4.  **Global Impact:** Visualizing which countries outside the US were hit hardest.
